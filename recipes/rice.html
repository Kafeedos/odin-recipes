For this recipe, bear in mind, I used Basmati rice. Other rice type cooking time will vary. Also, if you’re using long grain rice, you’ll need to parboil your rice first to speed up the cooking time. Also, do not skimp on White Pepper and Ginger, they really are important ingredients to help increase the flavor of Jollof rice. Butter is also important as it helps replenish the lost moisture and lushness during the high heat cooking process, especially if you’re working with Basmati rice.
